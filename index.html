What are you testing?

Here's how it works. You tell us what you're testing and we'll suggest methods for validating your design. We'll give you the leanest possible deliverable – in other words, the quickest deliverable that can still provide meaningful results. We'll also suggest some alternatives.

  • User interest

    Should I even spend time on this?

  • Content

    Is the content meaningful and useful?

  • User flows

    Can users do what they need to do?

  • Visual elements

    Is the visual design delightful and effective?

  • Calls to action

    Do they call enough attention?

  • Ease of use

    Are users making a lot of mistakes?

  • Emotional response

    How do users feel about the design?

  • Complex interactions

    What do users think of that gesture?
